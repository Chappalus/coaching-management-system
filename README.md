## What is Lynk Coach?

Lynk Coach replaces WhatsApp groups, Excel sheets, and paper registers with a single intelligent platform designed specifically for sports and activity-based coaching businesses.

Built for cricket academies, swimming schools, dance studios, martial arts dojos, gymnastics centers, music schools, yoga studios, fitness centers, and multi-sport complexes.

## The Problem

Most sports and extracurricular academies in India still run on:

- 📱 WhatsApp groups for scheduling and parent communication
- 📊 Excel spreadsheets for student records and fee tracking
- 📝 Paper registers for attendance
- 🧠 The owner's memory for everything else

This works until you hit ~50 students. After that, operational chaos becomes the bottleneck — not coaching quality.

## How Lynk Coach Solves It

### 🏟️ Facility-Aware Batch Scheduling
AI-assisted scheduling that manages court bookings, pool lanes, studio allocation, and ground availability — not just time slots. Weather-aware rescheduling automatically proposes alternatives when outdoor sessions are disrupted and notifies all affected parents via WhatsApp.

### 🎯 Trial Class & Lead Pipeline
Track every trial class from walk-in to enrollment. See conversion rates by sport, coach, time slot, and source. Automated follow-ups ensure no potential student falls through the cracks.

### 👤 Student & Member Management
Centralized profiles across all activities. A student doing both swimming and tennis appears in one profile, not two. Track enrollment history, batch assignments, attendance patterns, skill levels, medical notes, and sibling relationships.

### 📈 Skill-Based Progress Tracking
Purpose-built for sports and arts — not adapted from academic test scores. Track technique milestones, fitness metrics, competition readiness, and coach assessments. Auto-generate visual progress reports for parents.

### 💰 Automated Fee Collection & Reconciliation
Handle complex fee structures: seasonal packages, sibling discounts, multi-sport bundles, competition fees, equipment rentals. Smart reminders via WhatsApp. Real-time reconciliation across UPI, bank transfer, cash, and digital wallets.

### 👨‍🏫 Coach Management
Track schedules, specializations, certifications, and performance metrics. Automatic substitution suggestions when a coach is unavailable. Monitor which coaches drive the highest trial conversion and retention.

### 💬 WhatsApp-First Communication
Parents in India live on WhatsApp. Lynk Coach sends schedule changes, fee reminders, progress reports, and announcements through WhatsApp — not a separate app. Full message history captured in the platform.

### 📊 Analytics & Business Intelligence
Which sport has the highest retention? Which time slots are underutilized? Where are fee leakages? Which coaches drive the best outcomes? Actionable alerts, not just dashboards.

## Why We Built This

India's sports and extracurricular coaching market is growing at 15-20% annually. IPL, ISL, and PKL have created new career aspirations. Khelo India has boosted grassroots sports. Parents are investing in physical development and life skills like never before.But the technology powering these academies hasn't evolved in decades. We built Lynk Coach to change that — giving academy owners intelligent tools so they can focus on what matters: developing their students.

## Tech Stack

- **Scheduling Engine:** Constraint satisfaction problem (CSP) solver with hard constraints (facility availability, coach certification, safety) and soft constraints (parent preferences, batch optimization)
- **Payment Reconciliation:** Fuzzy matching on UPI transaction metadata + temporal proximity + amount pattern matching
- **Predictive Analytics:** Logistic regression on attendance + payment + engagement signals for dropout prediction
- **Communication:** WhatsApp Business API with structured message templates
- **Weather Integration:** Real-time weather API for automatic outdoor session rescheduling
